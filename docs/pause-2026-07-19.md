## The one real open item

**Ground truth is single-annotator across all five batches.** No batch has
been re-audited by an actual second annotator — only re-read against the
judge's own findings, which is not independent. Batches 2 through 5 each
caught the pass-1 annotator either missing or (batch 5) wrongly asserting a
real finding, so this is a known, measured weakness, not a theoretical one.

The highest-value next move is **one independent annotator on a stratified
sample of the traces that already exist** — not another dogfood batch.
Further batches chasing marginal κ are explicitly lower priority; coverage
stopped being the bottleneck when batch 5 closed the fair-test gap batch 4
left open.
